Trip Inventor Travel Special Fares

There are three types of Trip Inventor Special Fares:

  • Trip Inventor Special ZERO Reschedule Charges

    Reschedule requests need to reach Trip Inventor at least 36 hours for domestic flights and 72 hours for international flights before the original departure time. If the request does not come in this timeframe, then normal Airlines and Trip Inventor Travel Reschedule charges will be charged. However, Customers can also directly work with the airlines when the departure time is less than the above-mentioned timeframe. Any charges imposed by the airlines in this regard will need to be borne by the customer itself.

    Exact flight details of the new flight need to be provided for rescheduling requests - Only one option will be handled for rescheduling requests.

    Trip Inventor Travel takes 4 to 8 hours to respond during working hours for re-scheduled requests and is dependent on seat availability and airline response. In case the airline does not provide a timely or no response, Trip Inventor Travel cannot be held responsible for the same.

    Trip Inventor may charge certain service fees for the service rendered.

  • Trip Inventor Special ZERO Reschedule, 50% Cancellation Fees, Free Meal

    Reschedule details covered under Trip Inventor Special ZERO Reschedule Charges

    Airlines & Trip Inventor Cancellation Charges will be charged at 50% E.g. if the Airlines cancellation charges are Rs 3000 and Trip Inventor Charges Rs 499, then on this flight, the Airlines Cancellation Charges will be Rs 1500 and Trip Inventor Charges as Rs 249. All Cancellation requests need to reach Trip Inventor 36 hours on domestic flights and 72 hours on international flights before the original departure time. If the request does not come in this timeframe, then normal Airlines and Trip Inventor Cancellation charges will be charged. However, Customers can also directly work with the airlines when the departure time is less than the above-mentioned timeframe. Any charges imposed by the airlines in this regard will need to be borne by the customer itself.

    Free Meal is supposed to be provided by the airlines. However, if for any reason, airlines do not provide the Free MEAL, no compensation will be provided by Trip Inventor. MEAL Selection needs to be done by the customer on the airline’s website. Trip Inventor will not choose any MEAL type on the customer's behalf.

  • Trip Inventor Special ZERO Cancellation / Reschedule Fees, Free Meal

    Cancellation requests need to reach Trip Inventor at least 36 hours for domestic flights and 72 hours for international flights before the original departure time. If the request does not come in this timeframe, then normal/standard Airlines and Trip Inventor Cancellation charges will be charged.

    Please read above for Rescheduling of Flights & Free MEAL.
